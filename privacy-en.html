1. Summary Key Points

  • The Company may provide services without requiring registration/login, and in such cases, does not routinely collect "directly identifiable information" such as name, phone number, or address.
  • For games with IAP (In-App Purchases), payments are processed through Apple/Google's payment systems, and the Company does not directly collect or store payment method information such as card numbers.
  • For games with advertisements, advertising SDKs/ad providers may process advertising identifiers, device information, IP addresses, and ad interaction data for ad delivery and measurement (applicable games only).
  • Diagnostic/performance information (crash logs, etc.) may be processed for error response and service stabilization (when applicable).
  • Requests for access/deletion of personal information can be submitted through the Privacy Choices / Deletion Request section of this document.

2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to mobile game apps provided/distributed by the Company and related customer support channels (email, etc.). Features may vary by game (e.g., IAP, advertisements), and the following sections apply only when applicable.

3. Personal Information Collected and Collection Methods

3.1. Information the Company Does NOT Routinely Collect

The Company does not automatically collect or store the following personal directly identifiable information during gameplay:

  • Name, national ID number, contact information (phone number), address, bank/card information, precise location data, etc.
However, if a user directly provides such information via customer support email (e.g., including a phone number in a support inquiry), that information may be processed within the scope of handling the inquiry.

3.2. Customer Support (Email Inquiries) When Applicable

When users contact us via email, the following information may be processed for inquiry handling:

Category Items Purpose
Required (depending on inquiry) Email address, inquiry content Customer support, error verification and resolution
Optional Attachments (screenshots, etc.), device/OS/app version info (if provided by user), transaction ID/receipt/purchase token for purchase verification (if provided by user) Root cause analysis and dispute/refund inquiry response
Collection Method Directly submitted by user via email (dev.lhsg@gmail.com)

3.3. IAP (In-App Purchases) Applicable Games Only

Some games may sell digital goods/features through Apple App Store or Google Play's payment systems.

  • The Company does not directly collect or store payment method information such as card numbers.
  • For purchase delivery/restoration, fraud prevention, and customer support, purchase-related identification information (e.g., product ID, transaction ID, receipt/purchase token) provided by the store may be verified/processed in the app or server (applicable games only).
Example: Information that may be processed for IAP (when applicable)
  • Purchase product identifier (product ID), transaction/order identifier (transaction ID, etc.)
  • Receipt/purchase token (for purchase verification and restoration purposes)
  • Purchase processing status (success/failure), purchase timestamp (may remain in record form rather than exact time)
The actual processing scope may vary depending on the payment implementation method of the specific game (local verification/server verification, etc.) and store policies.

3.4. Advertisements (Banner/Interstitial/Rewarded, etc.) Applicable Games Only

Some games may display advertisements for free provision or to cover operational costs. In games with advertisements, advertising SDKs/ad providers may process the following information for ad delivery and measurement:

Information that advertising SDKs/ad providers may process (general examples)
  • Advertising Identifiers: iOS IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers), Android Advertising ID, etc. (may be limited by device/OS settings)
  • Device and App Information: Device model, OS version, language, app version, network status, IP address, etc.
  • Ad Interaction Information: Ad impressions/clicks/views/reward delivery status, etc.
  • Approximate Location Information: May be processed at an approximate regional level based on IP (not precise location)
In games without advertisements, the Company does not process the above information for advertising purposes (via advertising SDKs). Additionally, personalized advertising may be limited based on device settings/consent (according to OS policies).

3.5. Service Stabilization (Diagnostic/Performance Information) When Applicable

For error analysis and service stabilization, diagnostic/performance information (e.g., crash logs, error codes, performance metrics) may be collected, transmitted, and processed in some games. This information is generally used for error response and quality improvement, not for directly identifying individuals.

4. Purpose of Personal Information Processing

The Company processes personal information (when applicable) only for the following purposes:

  1. Customer Support and Complaint Handling (inquiry response, error resolution, dispute response)
  2. Purchase Feature Provision (purchase delivery/restoration, payment error handling, fraud prevention) IAP Applicable
  3. Ad Delivery and Measurement (ad impression/performance measurement, frequency capping, fraudulent click prevention, etc.) Ads Applicable
  4. Service Stabilization and Security (error analysis, service quality improvement, security incident prevention) When Applicable

5. Retention Period and Deletion

5.1. Principles

  • The Company processes personal information only for the period necessary to achieve its purpose and deletes it without delay after the purpose is achieved.
  • If retention is required by law, information may be retained for the period specified by applicable laws.

5.2. Retention Period by Type (General Standards)

Type Retention Standard Deletion Timing
Customer Support Inquiries (Email) Minimum period necessary for inquiry processing and dispute response Without delay after inquiry processing is complete (except when legally required to retain)
IAP-Related Information When Applicable Minimum period necessary for purchase delivery/restoration, fraud prevention, and customer support Without delay after purpose is achieved (or except when legally required to retain)
Diagnostic/Performance Information When Applicable Period necessary for error analysis and quality improvement Upon completion of analysis or when retention necessity expires

5.3. Deletion Methods

  • Electronic file format: Deleted in a manner that prevents recovery and reproduction
  • Printed materials (when applicable): Shredded or incinerated

6. Third-Party Provision and Sharing

6.1. Company Principles

The Company does not, in principle, provide users' personal information to third parties.

6.2. However, "Third Parties (Platform/SDK Providers)" may process information in the following cases:

  • Store Payments: For games with IAP, payments are processed through Apple/Google's payment systems.
  • Advertisements: For games with advertisements, advertising SDKs/ad providers may process data for ad delivery and measurement.
  • Legal Compliance: Information may be provided when required by law (such as lawful requests from law enforcement agencies).
Third-party services for advertising/analytics/payments may process data separately according to each provider's privacy policy.

7. Outsourcing of Personal Information Processing

The Company does not, in principle, outsource personal information processing to external parties.

However, if outsourcing occurs through the use of hosting/customer support/analytics tools during service operation, the outsourcing recipient and outsourcing tasks may be disclosed or notified in accordance with applicable laws.

8. International Transfer of Personal Information

The Company does not, in principle, transfer users' personal information overseas.

However, if third-party service providers for advertising/analytics/cloud/customer support tools are located overseas, personal information may be transferred overseas during the use of such services (applicable to specific games/features only). In such cases, the Company will notify or perform necessary procedures in accordance with applicable laws regarding the destination country, recipient, purpose, items, method, retention/use period, and opt-out method.

9. User Rights and How to Exercise Them

Users may exercise the following rights with respect to the Company:

  • Request to access, correct/delete, or suspend processing of personal information
  • Withdraw consent (for consent-based processing)

Rights may be exercised by emailing dev.lhsg@gmail.com. If the Company does not hold any personal information or identification is not possible, the reason will be explained.

10. Advertising and Personalized Advertising Choices (Opt-Out) Ads Applicable

In games with advertisements, advertising identifiers may be used to provide personalized advertising. Users can limit personalized advertising through device settings (the path may vary depending on OS version).

General Settings Examples
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking (or "App Tracking") to manage per-app allow/block settings
  • Android: Settings → Privacy (or Google settings) → Ads to limit ad personalization/reset Advertising ID (menu may vary by version)
When required consent (e.g., tracking permission) is needed according to OS/store policies, the Company may provide the consent process or limit personalized advertising (applicable games only).

11. Children's Personal Information

The Company does not, in principle, provide services directed at children under 13 years of age, and does not intentionally collect children's personal information.

If a specific game is directed at children or processes children's personal information, separate procedures such as parental consent will be applied and notified in accordance with applicable laws.

12. Security Measures

The Company applies the following measures to protect personal information (when applicable):

Access Control Internal Management Vulnerability Assessment Security Updates Transmission Encryption (When Applicable)
  • Access to personal information is granted only to authorized personnel and access is controlled.
  • Systems and software are kept up-to-date and security vulnerability assessments are performed.
  • Transmission protection measures are applied when transmitting personal information.
